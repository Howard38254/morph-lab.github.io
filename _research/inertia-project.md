## Overview

This research investigates how changes in mass distribution and inertia influence aircraft maneuverability and flight dynamics. 
Traditional aircraft rely primarily on aerodynamic forces for control, while in nature birds actively modify their inertia through 
wing morphing. This project aims to isolate and quantify the role of shifting inertia in flight by developing a morphing-wing 
UAV. This work hopes to see whether inertia can be used as an additional control mechanism to enhance agility and stability in flight.
